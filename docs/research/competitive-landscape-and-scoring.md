# Competitive Landscape and Scoring Position

## Positioning

CareerOS Student AI is a student placement-readiness workspace, not a job marketplace.

## Differentiation

- Evidence-linked rewrite suggestions instead of free-form text inflation
- Integrated ATS safety and JD scoring in one student flow
- Deterministic agent run with auditable steps
- Intel benchmark transparency for performance claims

## Competitive categories

1. Resume builders/checkers
2. Job boards and application platforms
3. Generic AI writing assistants

## Why this wedge

Most alternatives solve isolated steps. CareerOS focuses on end-to-end readiness:

- Parse resume structure
- Identify ATS risks
- Score against a target JD
- Suggest grounded improvements
- Export and apply

## Scoring integrity

- Formula lives only in `packages/scoring`
- Bench metrics require measured artifacts
- No fabricated readiness claims
